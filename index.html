About Me



I am currently a student at the University of Akron. I am studying computer science, programming, and web development. I have played soccer my entire life and I also try to golf frequently. In some of my other free time, I mess around with python and program raspberry pis to do cool things.

Fun fact: my brother taught me how to solve a rubik's cube and now I can solve the 2x2, 3x3, 4x4, 5x5, and 6x6 cubes. My dad and I also solved the 10x10 cube, but it takes a couple hours and I don't want to sit there that long...
